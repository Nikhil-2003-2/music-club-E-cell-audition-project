The music club at our college provides the students with an enviornment that helps them express themselves using musical ideas. If you like playing an instrument or you like to sing or even if you are just a music lover, then the music club is the perfect place for you to be in.

About 𝄞Tarang IIITG
The soul purpose of the music club is to promote the idea of having bright, cheerful and exciting experiences, in order to break the monotonicity of professional life and also to help students accquire a variety of skillset. The Music Club is a place where students come to jam, learn and make music of different flavours and genres. The club conducts a variety of activities throughout the year ranging from grand intra-campus shows to fierce musical competitions to productive musical workshops to participation in nation-wide competitions.

Events
Our club makes sure that we conduct a wide variety of events throughout the year ranging from small quizes that put to test, the knowledge of contestents to grand intra-campus shows to productive musical workshops. In our cultural fest "Yuvaan" the performances by our music club are the biggest attraction for the visitors. The band "Ataasi" which originated in our college was declared the winners of Unplugged, Alcheringa hosted by IIT Guwahati.
